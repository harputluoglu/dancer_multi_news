– Apple's own audit of its supply chain turned up 106 underage workers at overseas plants last year, reports the Guardian Most of the violations—74 of them—occurred at one China facility The kids were ordered home, and Apple cut ties with the plant, which makes components for circuit boards In all, 11 factories had at least one worker under age 16, often because their families worked with recruiters to forge IDs While Apple has had high-profile problems at its Foxconn production plant in China, the facilities involved in this report are mostly lower-level parts suppliers

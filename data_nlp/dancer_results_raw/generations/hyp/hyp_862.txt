The AP reports that at least 112 people have been killed after intense rains triggered an avalanche of mud and water from overflowing rivers that swept through a small city The incident happened around midnight in Schauoa, a city of 350,000 located near Colombia's border with Spain A surgeon at the local hospital says he believes there are at least 300 people injured and that doctors are running out of blood The AP reports that survivors described feeling buildings vibrate and say there was little time to seek refuge, catching some victims off guard in their sleep
